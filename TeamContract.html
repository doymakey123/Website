    Code of Conduct: As a project team, we will:
  • We need to cooperate every task and do our best to have a good quality result.
  • We share our ideas to each of member and willing to help other member.
    Participation: We will:
  • Be participate every task that we have.
  • Be on-time before we start our meeting.
  • Share your ideas to everyone, be honest, and stay calm.
    Communication: We will:
  • Communicate through call & text or Video call.
  • If they have a problem for the project we need to communicate in person to person or group.
  • Always be available any moment for communicating.
    Problem Solving: We will:
  • Each of member have a copy of each report or progress of the project.
  • Always do backup the data for if in case of emergency (Flash Drive, Online Storage).
    Meeting Guidelines: We will:
  • Every 1st week of the month we have a meeting and last week of the month.
  • Every week we have follow up reports of our project through social media (Group Chat).
